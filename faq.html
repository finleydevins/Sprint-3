FAQ

Question 1: How do new algorithms affect the content viewers see on their social media?

Answer: Different algorithms push different types of content towards a viewer's way based on what they like, view, and follow the most.

Question 2: How can I protect my privacy on my social media?

Answer: Using strong, complicated, unrelated codes and IDs could prevent fraud and hacking. Also, be cautious with what you share because nothing actually deletes forever!

Question 3: What are the potential risks associated with disruptive technology in social media?

Answer: Typical risks include the spread of false information, increased surveillance, privacy violations, mental health concerns due to algorithms, and severe data leaks.

Question 4: Does AI play a role in disruptive technology on social media?

Answer: Yes, AI plays a huge role in enabling advanced technologies through customizable images and information. The AI feature can help you from searching for a friend on the internet to solving a simple equation. Although there are many negatives to AI tools, they can be helpful on social media apps.

Question 5: How has technology changed the world through social media?

Answer: Social media has changed the way we communicate by making it easier to talk to people all over the world. It helps spread news quickly and allows people to share ideas and stand up for causes. Businesses also use it to reach more people. However, there are concerns about privacy and false information spreading.
